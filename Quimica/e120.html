Periodic Table: Element 120th
Unbinilium

    
       

One hundred and twentieth element of the Periodic Table

Unbinilium, also known as eka-radium or simply element 120, is the hypothetical chemical element in the periodic table with the symbol Ubn and the atomic number 120. Unbinilium and Ubn are the name and systematic symbol of IUPAC until a permanent name is decided. .
